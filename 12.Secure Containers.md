## Steps to Secure Containers

1. **Use distroless or minimal images** as the final image in multi-stage build → less chance of **CVE** *(Common Vulnerabilities & Exposures)* or security issues.

2. **Ensure networking is configured properly** — one of the most common reasons for security issues. If required, configure custom bridge networks & assign them to isolated containers.

3. **Use utilities "sync" to scan container images** for vulnerabilities before deployment.
