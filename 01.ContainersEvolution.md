## Containers — The Evolution

### The Problem with VMs (Resource Wastage)

| | VM1 | VM2 | VM3 | VM4 |
|--|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| **Allocated** | 10 GB / 25 CPU | 10 GB / 25 CPU | 10 GB / 25 CPU | 10 GB / 25 CPU |
| **Actually Used** | 8 GB / 15 CPU | 8 GB / 15 CPU | 8 GB / 15 CPU | 8 GB / 15 CPU |
| **Wasted** | ~2 GB / 10 CPU | ~2 GB / 10 CPU | ~2 GB / 10 CPU | ~2 GB / 10 CPU |

> **Hypervisor Total:** 40 GB / 100 CPU
> But each VM uses only 8 GB & 15 CPU — so resources & cost are wasted. This is why **Containers** came into the picture.
